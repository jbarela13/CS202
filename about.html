About Me.

I’m Jasmine Barela, I am a student athlete at Carroll College pursuing a degree in Business Management with a minor in Social Media Management and Public Relations. Balancing academics and athletics has taught me discipline, leadership, and hard work both on and off the field.

My journey to Carroll hasn’t been the easiest. I’ve undergone two ACL surgeries, each testing my patience, focus, and determination. Those challenges didn’t stop me they fueled me. I grew stronger, worked harder, and strived to become the best version of myself.

I’m passionate about combining my love for sports and business. My goal is to build a career where I can collaborate with athletes, teams, and brands to tell their stories, inspire others, and make an impact on the next generation.
